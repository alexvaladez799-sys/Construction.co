## 📐 Image Specifications

### Recommended Formats:
- **Photos:** WebP (primary), JPG (fallback)
- **Icons/Logos:** SVG (scalable), PNG (with transparency)
- **Graphics:** PNG or SVG

### Size Guidelines:
- **Hero Images:** 1920x1080px (Full HD)
- **Service Cards:** 800x600px (4:3 ratio)
- **Gallery Images:** 1200x900px (4:3 ratio)
- **Thumbnails:** 400x300px (4:3 ratio)
- **Team Photos:** 400x400px (1:1 ratio)
- **Icons:** 64x64px or SVG

### Optimization:
- Compress all images to <200KB for web
- Use lazy loading for below-fold images
- Provide @2x versions for retina displays
- Always include alt text for SEO

---

## 🎨 Image Style Guide

### Photography Style:
- **Professional & Clean** - No cluttered backgrounds
- **Well-Lit** - Natural lighting preferred
- **Action Shots** - Crew working, not just posing
- **Real Projects** - Actual company work, not stock photos
- **High Quality** - Sharp focus, good composition

### Color Treatment:
- Maintain consistent color grading across all photos
- Enhance blues and oranges (brand colors)
- Avoid overly saturated images
- Keep natural, realistic tones

## 🔍 Where to Get Images

### Priority 1: Your Own Photos
- Use photos from actual completed projects
- Take photos at current job sites
- Capture team at work
- **Benefit:** Authentic, unique, builds trust

### Priority 2: Client-Provided Photos
- Request photos from satisfied customers
- Get permission in writing to use
- Offer discount for photo usage rights

### Priority 3: Stock Photos (Last Resort)
**If using stock, make sure:**
- Photos look realistic, not staged
- Match Clovis/Central Valley aesthetic
- Workers are wearing proper safety gear
- Equipment looks professional

**Recommended Stock Sites:**
- Unsplash (free, high quality)
- Pexels (free, construction-specific)
- Shutterstock (paid, professional)
- iStock (paid, diverse options)

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Consistency is Key** - Use similar lighting/angles across similar photo types
2. **Show Your Face** - Include people in photos (humanizes the business)
3. **Safety First** - Always show proper safety equipment in action shots
4. **Clean Job Sites** - Photograph organized, professional work areas
5. **Golden Hour** - Shoot exterior photos during golden hour (sunrise/sunset)
6. **Get Signed Releases** - Always get permission to photograph people and properties
