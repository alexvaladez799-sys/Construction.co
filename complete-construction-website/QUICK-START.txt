Complete Construction Company - AI Website Builder Import Package

## Quick Import Instructions

1. **Unzip this package** to your computer

2. **Import Business Data:**
   - Navigate to `data/business-info.json`
   - Copy content into your AI builder's business info section

3. **Load Site Structure:**
   - Open `assets/sitemap.json`
   - Import into your page builder to create all pages automatically

4. **Add Content:**
   - Open `content/website-copy.md`
   - Copy/paste sections into corresponding pages

5. **Configure SEO:**
   - Open `seo/seo-metadata.json`
   - Apply meta tags to each page

6. **Apply Design:**
   - Open `assets/design-system.json`
   - Use color scheme and typography settings

7. **Add Reviews:**
   - Open `content/testimonials.json`
   - Import customer testimonials
